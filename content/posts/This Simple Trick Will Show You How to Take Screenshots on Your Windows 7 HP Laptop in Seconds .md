## Definition of a Screenshot

## Steps to Take a Screenshot on a Windows 7 HP Laptop

Taking a screenshot on your Windows 7 HP laptop is easy. Here’s how to do it:

1. Press the “Print Screen” key on your keyboard. This key is usually located near the top right corner of your keyboard.

2. Open a photo editing software, such as Microsoft Paint.

3. Paste the screenshot you just took by pressing “Ctrl” + “V” on your keyboard.
