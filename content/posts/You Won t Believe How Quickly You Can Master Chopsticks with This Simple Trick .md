### Definition of Chopsticks

Chopsticks are two long, thin sticks used to pick up food and move it from plate to mouth. They are usually made of wood, bamboo, or plastic and are held in the dominant hand while the other hand is used to guide the chopsticks.

## Trick to Mastering Chopsticks

### Step-by-step Instructions

The trick to mastering chopsticks is to use them like a pair of tongs. To start, hold the chopsticks in your dominant hand with your thumb on top and your index and middle fingers on the bottom. Then, use your index finger to press the top chopstick down while using your middle finger to press the bottom chopstick up. This will create a pincer-like grip that will allow you to pick up food easily. 

### Tips for Success

When using the pincer-like grip, it is important to keep your thumb and index finger close together. This will help you maintain a steady grip on the chopsticks. Additionally, practice is key. The more you practice, the more comfortable you will become with the pincer-like grip.
