<h2>Removing SOS from Your iPhone 14 Pro Max is Easier Than You Think</h2>

<h3>Introduction</h3>

SOS stands for “Save Our Souls” and is a feature on iPhones that allows you to quickly call emergency services. The iPhone 14 Pro Max is the latest model of Apple’s popular smartphone, and it comes with the SOS feature. However, if you don’t need the SOS feature, you may want to remove it from your phone. In this blog post, we’ll discuss how to do just that. 

<h3>Steps to Remove SOS from iPhone 14 Pro Max</h3>

Removing SOS from your iPhone 14 Pro Max is actually quite simple. Here are the steps you need to take: 

<h4>Step 1: Open the Settings App</h4>

The first step is to open the Settings app on your iPhone 14 Pro Max. This can be done by tapping the Settings icon on your home screen. 

<h4>Step 2: Select Emergency SOS</h4>

Once you’re in the Settings app, you’ll need to select the “Emergency SOS” option. This can be found in the “Emergency” section of the app. 

<h4>Step 3: Toggle the Auto Call Option to Off</h4>

Once you’ve selected the “Emergency SOS” option, you’ll need to toggle the “Auto Call” option to “Off”. This will disable the SOS feature on your iPhone 14 Pro Max. 

<h4>Step 4: Confirm Your Selection</h4>

Once you’ve toggled the “Auto Call” option to “Off”, you’ll need to confirm your selection. You can do this by tapping the “Confirm” button at the bottom of the screen. 

<h3>Conclusion</h3>

In conclusion, removing SOS from your iPhone 14 Pro Max is easy. All you need to do is open the Settings app, select the “Emergency SOS” option, toggle the “Auto Call” option to “Off”, and confirm your selection.
